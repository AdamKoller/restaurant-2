About

Fine dining with the most freshest and delicious ingredients should not break the bank. Here at Naomi Rae's, our philosophy is that the experience is what makes a restaurant memorable, not the prices on the menu. How do we achieve this? By eliminating the middle man and sourcing everything from local farms around the St.Louis area.



Opened in 2015, celebrities, St.Louis visitors and loyal neighborhood residents enjoy two distinct locations: Kirkwood and Clayton. Aside from the comfortable and inviting atmosphere, Naomi Rae's offers classic cocktails, an extensive wine list, premium steaks, fresh fish, and delicate desserts.

Our Recognition

Riverfront Times awarded Naomi Rae's 'Best New Restaurant' in 2015 and is the winner of three James Beard Foundation Awards.
